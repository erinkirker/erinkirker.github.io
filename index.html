About Me

I was born in 2015 in the lovely suburb of Wantirna, Victoria to Ralph and Vanilla. I am one of six, though I am adamant that I am the favourite child. At the tender age of eight weeks, I was scooped up in the arms of Erin and jetted off to my new life in the suburbs.

I like causing trouble; always have ever since I arrived. Within my first few days I had already destroyed the toys I'd been given, bitten everyone's feet repeatedly and cried until I got my way. Not to mention the peeing.

Eventually I was introduced to Sam,, the current boss dog. He was definitely miffed at my arrival but a bat of my long eyelashes soon fixed that. Who could resist such a pretty face?

Nowadays you'll find me nestled in Erin's bed or ripped up any combination of plants, furniture or toys. I love to play and sleep in strange positions. I'm a dog of the world, and nobdody can stop me.

Unless I'm on a leash, I guess.


Fun Facts:
  • My birthday is Jan 27
  • My favourite toy was a stuffed frog, until I destroyed it
  • I like to sleep on my back, with one paw in the air
  • I am afraid of the beach, but like swimming in my paddling pool
  • I once got stuck in another dog's collar
  • I am a giant princess
